Places I Have Visited

GOA

The Island Of India
I Visited In 2018 Winter

Goa is one of the most beautiful places in India situated in the western coast of Arabian Sea. Baga Beach Is A Great Beach. Ibis Style Hotel Experience Is Great. Catching up the sunset in the scenic beaches, holding up the hands of your loved one, taking a long relaxed walk on the sands and of course, not to forget about indulging in some rich sea food, it is a pure bliss! What more can you ask for on a holiday??? Goa is the perfect destination for people of all the age.

CARS 2
Collision Of World's !

Star racecar Lightning McQueen and his friend Mater journey abroad to participate in a Grand Prix race. The path to the championship becomes troublesome when Mater gets involved in espionage. This is one of the most interesting and unique sequels that you've ever made for a movie, and for once it didn't take you forever. I get why you people hate this film because it's a spy movie and not a racing movie like Cars 1 and 3, but that doesn't make it terrible. I love how Mater becomes the main character in this movie and must uncover the villains responsible for the World Grand Prix being a set up for the popular racers like Lightning, and i also loved the introduction to Holly and Finn going undercover with Mater to expose all the bad guys, and the way he returned after Lightning turned him down was so breath-taking and terrifying (in a good way) with the bomb stuck to his engine. I would appreciate Pixar because this movie because of how amazing it is. It might honestly be my favorite of the Cars series and I Will Give This Movie 5 Out Of 5 Stars. #Blockbuster #Wellmade #Superdirection
